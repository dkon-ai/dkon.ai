DKON — SMS Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This SMS/MMS messaging service is operated by Rick Barraza (dkon.ai) as a private, family-facing personal assistant. This policy explains how information is handled for the text-messaging program.

No sharing of mobile information

We do not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers — or any SMS opt-in consent — with any third parties or affiliates for any purpose. Phone numbers are used solely to send and receive messages within this service.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies; the service is conversational and reply-based.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply, according to your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-out & help

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.
