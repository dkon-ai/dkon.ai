Messaging Terms & Conditions

Personal notification service operated by Rick Barraza, Boise, Idaho. Last updated July 2026.

Program

DKON is a personal notification service. When you enroll, you receive short coordination and reminder text messages — schedule changes, event reminders, running-late notices, and confirmations — from Rick Barraza.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies and is low: approximately a few messages per week.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply. DKON does not charge for the messages; your mobile carrier's standard rates apply.

How to opt in

Check the consent box and submit your number at dkon.ai/msgs, or ask Rick in person. Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

How to opt out

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You will receive one confirmation and no further messages unless you re-enroll.

Help

Reply HELP for help, or email msgs@dkon.ai.

Carriers

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your information is handled per our Privacy Policy. Mobile information is never shared with or sold to third parties.
