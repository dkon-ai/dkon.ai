Privacy Policy — DKON Messaging

Personal notification service operated by Rick Barraza, Boise, Idaho. Last updated July 2026.

This policy covers the DKON personal notification service, which sends coordination and reminder text messages to family members who enroll at dkon.ai/msgs or in person.

No mobile information will be shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third party.

What we collect

Only what enrollment requires: your name and the mobile number you provide, and the fact that you gave consent to receive messages.

Why we collect it

Solely to send you the personal coordination and reminder messages you asked to receive. We do not use it for advertising, and we do not sell, rent, or trade it.

Who it is shared with

No one, except the messaging carrier strictly required to deliver a message to your phone. It is never shared with any third party for marketing, and never sold.

How to opt out

Reply STOP to any message to stop all messages. Reply HELP for help. You may also ask Rick directly to remove your number.

Data retention

We keep your number only while you are enrolled. When you opt out, your number is removed from the active list.

Contact

Questions about this policy: msgs@dkon.ai.
